Site policy

Privacy Policy

This page explains how PeptideSuppliers.org may collect, use, and share information, including through email contact, affiliate links, and future advertising tools such as Google AdSense.

Information

What may be collected

If you contact the site by email, the information you choose to send may be retained so the message can be answered and referenced later.

Usage

How information is used

Information is used to respond to inquiries, review supplier submissions, and improve the content and clarity of the site.

Cookies and ads

Advertising technology

If Google AdSense or similar advertising tools are used on the site, third-party vendors including Google may use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this site or other websites.

Third parties

External sites and affiliate tracking

This site links to external supplier websites. Those sites may use their own analytics, cookies, or affiliate tracking. Their privacy practices are separate and should be reviewed on their own sites.

Choices

Managing cookies and personalization

Users can control cookies through browser settings. If Google serves ads on the site, users may also review ad personalization settings through Google's ad settings tools.

Contact

Questions about privacy

Questions can be sent to thepeptideforum@gmail.com.

Ad policy readiness

Google advertising disclosure

If ads are enabled, PeptideSuppliers.org intends to maintain a privacy policy that discloses cookie use, third-party advertising, and related data collection practices.
