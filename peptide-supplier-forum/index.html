Forum rules

Keep the discussion evidence-based and useful.

The goal of the forum is to compare visible supplier trust signals, not to turn the site into an unmoderated claims board. Strong discussions should point back to supplier pages, shipping language, or documentation that other readers can verify themselves.

Guidelines

What belongs here

  • Share supplier page findings, not unverifiable rumors
  • Keep discussion focused on research-use storefront signals
  • Avoid medical advice and unsafe claims
  • Call out policy or shipping updates clearly
  • Use links to supplier pages whenever possible
