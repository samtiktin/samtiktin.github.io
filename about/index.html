About the directory

A cleaner standard for supplier discovery.

PeptideSuppliers.org is structured to help research-oriented visitors understand what makes a supplier feel credible before they ever click through to a listing.

Mission

Clarity beats clutter.

Many supplier pages blend useful trust signals with vague claims. This site is designed to make testing visibility, documentation quality, and research-use positioning easier to scan at a glance.

That means cleaner information architecture, straightforward language, and pages that feel more editorial than noisy.

Principles

What the site is built around

Transparency over hype.

Educational support before conversion pressure.

Simple, trustworthy presentation on every page.

Research-use-only framing kept visible and consistent.
