Frequently asked questions

Straight answers for first-time visitors.

A solid FAQ helps the site feel finished and gives visitors context before they rely on any listing or supplier recommendation.

Question

What should a credible supplier page include?

Clear product descriptions, visible testing documentation, recent COAs, and consistent research-use-only language are the basics.

Question

Why do COAs matter?

They are one of the clearest public trust signals a supplier can provide, especially when the documents are readable and easy to access.

Question

Does PeptideSuppliers.org sell products directly?

This version of the site is structured as an informational and directory-style resource, not as a direct ecommerce storefront.

Question

Can the site grow beyond static pages?

Yes. It can expand into deeper supplier profiles, comparison pages, and guides while still staying lightweight.

Question

How should suppliers be compared?

Compare documentation visibility, transparency, site clarity, shipping communication, and how consistently research-use-only framing is maintained.

Question

Is this site ready for GitHub Pages?

Yes. These pages are plain static HTML and CSS, so they can publish directly without a build step.
