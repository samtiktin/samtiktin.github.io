Submission page

Submit a supplier for review

Suppliers and visitors can use this page to suggest new additions for the directory. The stronger the submission, the easier it is to decide whether a listing belongs on the site.

Where to send it

Email submissions

Send supplier submissions to thepeptideforum@gmail.com.

Email a submission
What to include

Basic submission checklist

  • Supplier name and website URL
  • Main peptide categories or compounds carried
  • Where COAs or testing documents can be found
  • Shipping region and fulfillment notes
  • Why the supplier belongs in the directory
What helps most

Stronger submissions are easier to review

The best submissions link directly to relevant product pages, testing pages, policy pages, and any visible documentation that supports the supplier’s case.

Important limit

Submission does not guarantee listing

The site stays editorial. A supplier may be reviewed, added to the finder, or declined based on visible trust signals and overall fit with the directory.
